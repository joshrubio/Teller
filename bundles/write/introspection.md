## Focus

Introspection is where themes surface as texture. Load themes node first — motifs (omission, Odem, registration, resistance vs. stasis) should appear in the grain of observation, not as named concepts.

POV character's voice governs register:
- Oliver notices what's missing before what's present

## Constraint

Never state a theme directly in prose. If it can be cut without losing narrative information, the theme is not doing its job.
