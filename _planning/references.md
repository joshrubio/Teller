# Reference Works — Stylistic Calibration

Planning reference. Load when reviewing tone or setting up new arcs — not per-scene.

## Manga / Anime

| Work | What to take |
|---|---|
| **Naruto** | Power system with clear rules and visible costs. Personal bonds as tactical decision drivers. Training as process, not ellipsis. Identity under institutional pressure. |
| **Hunter x Hunter** | Nen = explicit conditions, contracts, restrictions — closest parallel to Odem. Tactical intelligence above brute force. Antagonists with genuine logic. Deliberately slow rhythm before the strike. |
| **Jujutsu Kaisen** | Cursed energy as invisible urban force — direct parallel to Potestades. Horror + action without separating registers. Cost of existing in a broken world without overdramatizing. Death and loss without cushioning. |

**What Ledger of Domains takes:** visible system with real cost, institutional environment with its own agenda, characters learning under irreversible pressure.

**What Ledger of Domains does NOT do:** power fantasy, unlimited escalation, clean emotional resolution.
