# POV: Asha — Tension

## Canonical gestures

- Arms crossed when observing — active analysis posture, not defensive.
- Direct gaze that equalizes, does not evaluate: peer treatment.
- Stands when the conversation is over for her, without prior signals.
- Looks at objects (not people) when making technical observations.

## Speech patterns

- Closes arguments without inviting reply: "Básico.", "Probablemente.", "Ya."
- Process language: variables, inputs, outputs, results.
- Does not add emotional context to technical observations.

## Analogies used

- "variable de entrada / variable de salida / predecir la salida" — Cap 12
- "resistencia del objeto antes de la estructura del Dominio" — Cap 16
- "archivo que ha terminado de cargar" (Oliver describing her signature) — Cap 13

## Watch for

<!-- canonical -->
- **"la atención directa que Oliver había aprendido a reconocerle — no evaluativa, sino igualatoria"** — used verbatim in Cap 13. In later caps: NEVER repeat verbatim. Trust the reader knows her gaze.
- "la atención X de alguien que Y" — globally eliminated. Do not reintroduce.
