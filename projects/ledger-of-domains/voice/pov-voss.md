# POV: Voss — Architecture/Space

## Base tone

Burocrático-inquietante, calculado.

## Canonical gestures

- Does not turn when speaking if he can avoid it — processes from oblique angle.
- Involuntarily counts distances and proportions. **REGISTRO:** 104m del corredor (Cap 9) — do not repeat.
- Rests hand on surfaces as active spatial reading, not physical support.
- Leans slightly when the conversation is under his control.

## Speech patterns

- Pauses before information that shifts dynamics — calculated, not nervous.
- Exactly sufficient response.
- "Calcular" and "archivar" as internal process metaphors.
- Ends conversations without closing formality.

## Analogies used

- "ciento cuatro metros" del corredor AMAT-NA — Cap 9
- "variable cuyo peso no había calculado todavía" — Cap 9
- "calcular cuánto vale la respuesta" — Cap 11

## Watch for

- "con la atención específica de alguien que verifica que la pregunta era sincera" — overused Caps 9–12. Max one per chapter.
- "archivó como variable" — do not repeat literally.
