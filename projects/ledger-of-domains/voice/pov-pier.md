# POV: Pier — Weight

## Canonical gestures

- Movimientos elegidos, no reflejos: cada desplazamiento de peso es consciente. Verbos de decisión, no de reflejo.
- Objetos colocados con exactitud. **Use ONCE per scene**, then trust the established pattern.
- Minimal forward lean when something genuinely matters to him.
- Stands with economy: chooses when and from where.

## Speech patterns

- Teaching mode: proposition → implication → close. No filler.
- "El cuerpo" as frequent subject.
- Rarely asks direct questions — frames statements and waits.
- Calls Oliver by name before information that matters.

## Domain descriptions

Must sound **theoretical**, not intuitive. (Park = intuitive. They must never converge.)

## Analogies used

- "saber exactamente dónde está la gravedad en cada momento y decidir ir con ella" — Cap 14
- Movement "elegido", each weight shift "calculado, no con esfuerzo sino con conciencia" — Cap 14
- "no desperdiciaría ni un milímetro de distancia" — Cap 14

## Watch for

- Three similar descriptions of precise movement in same chapter — ONE per scene only.
- "la precisión de alguien que..." — overused. Prefer concrete action.
- Measurements in every physical interaction — only when the number matters.
