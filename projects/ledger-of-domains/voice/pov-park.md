# POV: Park — Strength

## Canonical gestures

- Notes proportions and angles of space he enters.
- Positions body calculating action angles before needing them.
- **Slight head tilt before speaking** — minimal, not dramatic. Established Cap 24.
- Does not fill silences — uses them as information.

## Speech patterns

- Dry, economic. Warnings without explaining the reason.
- Dark humor at close of high-tension situations.
- With Oliver: more words than he uses with anyone else.
- The pause before responding is not hesitation: it is filtering — selects how much to say.

## Domain descriptions

Must sound **intuitive**, not theoretical. Park does not explain mechanics — he acts. (Pier = theoretical. They must never converge.)

## Watch for

- Never "runs to charge" the Domain. See `system/strength`.
- Do not fill silences he would not fill.
