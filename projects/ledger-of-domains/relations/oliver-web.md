# Oliver's Relationship Web

## Oliver / Park

**Model:** David and Jonathan (1 Samuel 18–20). Park = Jonathan (protective, strategic, took personal risk). Oliver = David (direct, genuine). Loyalty that does not calculate cost.

**History:**
- Paired as "the guys" at the internship company (Arc 1).
- Park almost let him die (Cap 4 — decision that weighs through all of Arc 2).
- Oliver's first question from custody was about Park, not himself.
- Park handed him over — not betrayal, but politics. That weight is unpaid.
- Cap 20: reunion at the Centro. "Lo que ocurrió en esos once minutos no es algo que yo pueda devolverte." No resolution yet.

**Current state:** Working together. The debt and the loyalty coexist without being named.

**Pending tension:** Park doesn't know Voss placed him at the Centro ("necesito saber si hay alguien prestando atención"). When he finds out — implied Cap 21 — it changes how he reads his own presence there.

## Oliver / Voss

**Dynamic:** comprehension without empathy. Voss understands Oliver's potential better than anyone — which makes him more dangerous than any containment agent.

**History:**
- Caps 7–9: Voss reads the file, enters the cell, negotiates the Shared Evaluation Protocol.
- Cap 12: "Los otros casos copiaron la amenaza. Oliver copió la curiosidad — copiará todo lo que encuentre interesante."
- Cap 24: Voss's birthday. Oliver discovers the group has celebrated it since the first year (Pier's tradition). Voss arrives for twenty minutes, chooses Yusuf before the group — they laugh together, first time anyone sees either of them do it.
- Cap 35: Oliver finds him in the administrative corridor. Voss gives the honest answer about Sisi's protocol — "Ahora mismo, no va a cambiar" — and adds without being asked: he's been pushing the Geneva group for nine months. Not a promise; a complete diagnosis.
- Cap 37: Voss seals the Centro. Looks at Oliver like someone who just watched the system he designed fail. Asks "¿Qué te dijo esta noche?" Oliver shares the what (Elise, 11 years, same decision) but lets Voss assume contact was during the breach. Does not say when — does not say the plaza. Voss: "Eso es una clase de argumento." Requests "everything" for when they reach Sion, with Maren present.

**Pending tension:** Oliver promised "everything" in Sion — but the plaza (9 days) is not in that everything. When Voss finds out, it changes what he thinks of Oliver's margin of error.

## Oliver / Maren

**Dynamic:** most genuine alliance inside AMAT. Maren asks "¿cómo está?" with real intent.

**History:**
- Longitudinal evaluation: 11 years studying 3 Registro cases without a living subject.
- Cap 17: enters the patio breaking protocol — something in the deliberate copy moved her.
- Cap 25: identifies first symptom of long-term cost. Oliver does not resolve it yet.
- Cap 28: after the session with Sisi, Maren takes Oliver to a lateral corridor off-camera. Does not explicitly tell him to continue the arrangement — implies it. Planted visible doubt about Hana (waited for the door to close before looking at Oliver). Direct warning: there are real consequences if Oliver does not manage information better; she needs direction so she can work. Oliver: "Mira la sesión de hoy — pero mira también cómo llegué a ella." Implicit agreement: complicity, not instruction.

**Pending tension:** Maren's family = AMAT agents (established Cap 27). Adds layer to her institutional loyalty vs. loyalty to Oliver as subject. Hana as possible unsafe variable — planted, unconfirmed.

## Oliver / Sisi

**Dynamic:** the only person in the Centro whose Domain is invisible to him. Oliver connects with her differently — not by what he sees, but by what he cannot see.

**History:**
- Cap 22: first time Oliver sees someone completely opaque. Sisi's distrust of AMAT: founded.
- Cap 23: first real exterior exchange.
- Cap 24: Domain game in the south patio. Sisi actively observes Oliver during all three rounds. When Oliver turns his head after the accidental Ancla copy, she was already there. Nods once, returns to Asha's conversation. Close: Oliver/Sisi/Riku in the corridor — Sisi says something personal ("no sabía si lo que sentía era mío o prestado"). Riku leaves them alone. First time alone without tactical tension.
- Cap 27: Oliver describes how he sees everyone's Odem except hers. First genuine moment.
- Cap 28: Four days later, Sisi goes to Oliver's room and confesses using the Domain in the session — predisposition, not control. Also confesses what weighs on her: she feels the exact moment the other's resistance gives. Oliver: "Gracias por decírmelo." No decision. No resolution.
- Cap 34: Sisi discovers her Centro regime is different. Oliver does not tell her about Martin. Commitment: "De acuerdo." First time she knocks on his door.
- Cap 35: Oliver arrives with Voss's answer. Sisi already knows — she read him. Coat on. Backpack on the floor. "Me quedo", says Oliver. "Entonces ya sé lo que necesitaba saber."

**Active commitment (Cap 33):** Oliver promises to protect her from Tomás's fate. He cannot keep it.

**Departure (Cap 36):** Sisi leaves with Kaspar. Not force — informed decision. Kaspar represents 40 years of life outside the system. The conversation with Kaspar ("¿Qué me pasa a mí?" / "No lo sé") tips the scale: a man who had come to convince her would have softened the answer. He does not.

**"Vuelve" (Cap 36 — Book 2 hook):** Oliver arrives at the north wing as Kaspar and Sisi are about to cross the last wall. "Todavía a tiempo", says Kaspar. Oliver looks at Sisi. Says: "Vuelve." She holds it for a moment. No answer. Closes her hand around Tomás's wrist and disappears. No promise from Sisi. The request stays open.

## Oliver / Kaspar

**Why Kaspar seeks Oliver:** the only living person with real perspective on what the Registro does to a person long-term. Whether his "help" is genuine or instrumental: intentionally open.

**History:**
- Cap 33: meeting in the village plaza, post-mission. Kaspar presents as "Martin" (Appearance Domain: brown hair, ~35 years). Knows things he should not know about the cost of the Registro. Oliver does not report to Park. Nine days of secrecy.
- Cap 35: Oliver recognizes Kaspar's signature in the north corridor — same as the plaza, the one he's carried nine days without depositing. Kaspar offers to leave with him. Oliver does not respond. Kaspar passes and continues toward the residential wing.
- Cap 36: Kaspar already has Sisi ready to cross. Oliver arrives. "Todavía a tiempo." Oliver says "Vuelve" to Sisi. Kaspar does not interfere.
- Cap 37: Kaspar left a note for Oliver on his desk. In English — deliberate distance. Elise: previous Registro carrier. Same decision as Oliver. Kaspar observed her eleven years. "Cuando necesites saber lo que viene, ya sabes cómo encontrarme." Oliver does not share it with AMAT. Keeps it in his jacket's outer pocket.

**Elise (Book 2 hook):** first Registro carrier documented by Kaspar. Made the same decision as Oliver. Kaspar does not tell Oliver what happened to her — only that he observed her eleven years. Oliver shares the what with Voss (Voss: "Eso es una clase de argumento"). Omits the when.
