# Odem

The foundational energy of the system.

## Definition

The breath of God present in all created things. The common substance underlying both Potestades and human Domains.

**Theological reference (internal background):** Term from Archaic German (Luther, Genesis 2:7) — *"blies ihm den Odem des Lebens"* — for the Hebrew **Neshama** (נְשָׁמָה): the specific breath God breathes into Adam. Not neutral energy — **borrowed breath**. What carries Odem exists; what releases it, returns to dust.

## Mechanics

- Invisible to almost everyone.
- Manifests as filaments, lines of tension between things — the breath-web holding reality together.
- What stops resisting, stops "pushing back," releases its Odem and can be erased.

## Nature

Not physically measurable energy — metaphysical substance. Closer to the Platonic soul (ψυχή). Material things can exist without Odem (blank nodes: physically present, metaphysically absent). Human Domains express *through* physical channels but operate on the same underlying spiritual substrate.

## Oliver and Odem

Oliver does not see energy. He sees *how far each thing's Odem extends* — and the points where that breath is missing.

## Limits of Odem perception

| Limit | Reason |
|---|---|
| Blindfold = total shutdown | Registro activates through vision |
| Normal darkness = normal limit | Odem overlays vision, does not replace it. Does not see through walls |
| Signal noise | Multiple active Domains simultaneously degrade reading |
| Cannot read himself | Sees structure of others' Domains, not his own |
| Does not see intent, only structure | Reads the pattern — not the user's emotion or motivation |
| Does not see past states | Only the present |
| Passive signature ≠ enough to copy | Needs active Domain to read structure |

## Morning phenomenon

Contours are sharper in the mornings from accumulated noise: after hours of perceiving passive signatures, the system saturates. Sleep functions as a reset.
