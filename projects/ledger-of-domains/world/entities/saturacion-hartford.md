# Saturación — Hartford Entity

## Classification

| | |
|---|---|
| **AMAT designation** | Unclassified (no catalog entry) |
| **Observed level** | Presents as Nivel 3 — actual level unclear, possibly evolving |
| **Origin** | Generated from Odem residue left by the 1952 Hartford Principado event |
| **First contact** | BSA Cap 1 (silhouette, north sector) / BSA Cap 2 (direct contact) |

## Nature

Not a standard generated Potestad. Standard Potestades are nodes of concentrated Odem that form spontaneously in zones of high saturation — they have a defined radius, a readable signature, a stable form. Saturación is something different: an entity born from and composed of the same Odem that saturated Hartford's ground in 1952.

She is not in the zone. She IS the zone's Odem, self-organized into agency.

This is why AMAT's localizadores did not read her: the localizadores detect Odem differential against background. Saturación registers as zero differential because she is the background.

## Domain — Saturación

**Odem mass:** she carries an extraordinary concentration of Odem within and around her at all times. This mass of Odem is her body, her weapon, and her means of presence. She does not channel Odem from an external source — she is the Odem.

**Spatial presence:** she can materialize her body (or any part of it) at any point within the Odem field she carries. Practically: she appears and disappears within the area her Odem covers. This is not teleportation — it is her choosing which part of her distributed Odem to make physical and coherent. Hendricks's Domain suppression field stopped this in Cap 2 by preventing active channeling, forcing her to maintain a single coherent physical form.

**Force amplification:** her strikes carry the full weight of her Odem mass. A fist that appears to have the mass of a teenager's arm delivers force proportional to her actual Odem density — many multiples of what the body suggests. This is passive (accumulated Odem mass), not active channeling — Hendricks's suppression field did not reduce her strike force.

**Physical damage response:** her body is maintained by Odem coherence, not biological function. Gunshots disrupt the Odem structure locally. She registers this as information — she can feel the disruption — but it does not produce pain or incapacitation in the biological sense. A bullet to the heart makes her bleed (the Odem structure is interrupted) but does not stop her.


## Consciousness

She is conscious. This is anomalous for a generated Potestad.

Observable evidence (BSA Cap 2):
- After throwing Cho: paused to examine her hand. Processed the sensation of Odem force transfer.
- After receiving the shot to the chest: paused to assess the disruption. Not pain response — information processing.
- Chose targets non-randomly: attacked Park (who had no active Domain signal to suppress) in preference to Hendricks (active suppression field) and the veterans.
- When the secondary underground presence was collapsed: she stepped back, registered the loss, then withdrew gradually rather than abruptly. Deliberate retreat, not route.

Her consciousness is new. She is discovering what she is and what she can do in real time. Her learning curve across Cap 2's combat was visible: the second exchange with Park showed tactical adjustment that the first attack did not.

## Evolution hypothesis (BSA Arc 1)

Standard AMAT understanding: Potestades generated in residual-saturation zones begin at a fixed Nivel determined by ambient Odem density and slowly degrade as the saturation disperses. Saturación's behavior suggests the inverse: she is accumulating, stabilizing, developing agency.

**Cap 4 implication:** Park's reading of her as a fragment of something larger is correct. She is not a Potestad that evolved from Nivel 4 to Nivel 3. She is the 1952 Principado's Odem becoming autonomous — and the rate of that becoming is accelerating.

## Appearance

Presents as a young person: approximately 16–17 years old, slight build, white hair (not decolorized — white from the root), face that has not settled into its final form. The form is not fixed — it is the shape the accumulated Odem chose for coherent physical expression. Why this shape: unknown.

## AMAT catalog status

No entry. The localizador has recordings of the contact events. The system cannot match the signature because Saturación's signature is identical to Hartford's baseline Odem saturation — no differential to catalog.

First AMAT record that can be associated with her: the 1952 Principado event file, pages 74+ (classified EU-GINEBRA). What those pages contain about what the Principado left behind when it withdrew: unknown to Park, to Holt, to anyone with Hartford clearance.
