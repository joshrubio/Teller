# AMAT — Agency for the Monitoring of Anomalous Threats

## Name history

| Period | Name | Phase |
|---|---|---|
| ~1720s–1883 | *(unnamed)* | Private epistolary network. Zinzendorf / Moravian Brethren as founding node. Framework: Ephesians 6:12. |
| ~1883–1939 | **All Myths Are True** | Internal identifier only. No institutional structure. Geneva hub emerges during WWI. |
| ~1939–1945 | **Agency for the Monitoring of Anomalous Threats** | Formally constituted during WWII. Geneva Director appointed. Field rank system established. |

## Character

- Field agents = "hammer"; most situations = "nails."
- Internal terminology: **Potestad** (anomalous entity), **Dominio** (human power), **Domain** (entity's isolated space).
- Operate undercover in normal companies; disuasion missions before containment.
- Generous budgets; pragmatic morality.
- Must feel dangerous, not just bureaucratic.

## Authority hierarchy

```
Director de Ginebra
    ├── Regional Directors (e.g. Cartwright — AMAT-NA)
    │       └── Division Heads (e.g. Park's father — Korea Division)
    │               └── Field Agents (e.g. Park — Rango 4→2)
    └── Voss / Brenner (EU — Geneva central)
            └── Centro Valais (Maren, staff)
```

**Brenner:** Coordinator of Operational Assignments, AMAT-EU Geneva. Signs transfer orders. Deployment authority that Voss does not have. Potential conflict in Arc 3 if Voss wants to retain Oliver and Brenner wants to deploy him.

## Agent rank system

Ascending: **Rango 4 → 3 → 2 → 1 → S**

> Reflects operational authorization and documented autonomous resolution capacity — not raw power. An agent can have Rango 2 capability and remain Rango 3 if they have not had the mission to prove it.

| Rank | Operational name | Authorization | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **4** | Agente de Campo | Nivel 4 ops. Nivel 3 accompanied under supervision | Entry point. Most agents never advance. Park at start. |
| **3** | Agente Senior | Autonomous Nivel 3. Nivel 2 in team. First command authority on mission | Requires clean solo Nivel 3 resolution as verified lead. |
| **2** | Especialista Operativo | Autonomous Nivel 2. Nivel 1 in team under Rango 1 direction. Briefing authority | Cartwright recommends; Geneva confirms. |
| **1** | Agente de Élite | Autonomous Nivel 1. Principado approach protocol. No scale limit with team | Achievable ceiling. Dozens in AMAT globally. Voss = Rango 1. Cartwright = Rango 1. |
| **S** | Agente Primordial | Direct Principado contact protocol. No operational superior in field. Coordinates exclusively with Geneva Director | Fewer than 10 in the world. Not earned by missions — direct Director designation. Ji-ho (Park's uncle) = Rango S. |

## Institutional positions (outside operational scale)

| Position | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Division Head | Responsible for a national or regional branch. Implied minimum operational rank: 1. | Park's father — Korea Division |
| Regional Director | Authority over multiple divisions. Approves Rango 2 with Geneva confirmation. | Cartwright — AMAT-NA |
| Geneva Director | Maximum authority. Approves Rango 1 and Rango S directly. | Unnamed |
