# Hartford Zone

## Classification

Principado imprint — active since 1952 withdrawal.

## Location

Hartford, Connecticut, USA.

## Status

Active impregnated zone. AMAT-NA perimeter under environmental remediation cover. The Principado withdrew — destination unknown. Generates Nivel 3–4 Potestades periodically.

## Narrative function

- Park's M4 (Hartford) takes place here — team operation under special zone classification. Credit shared; does not count as clean solo resolution.
- The zone itself is the threat, not a single entity — it's a permanent scar in the Odem of the area.
- Distorts how human Domains function within the perimeter.

## AMAT protocol

Long-term perimeter monitoring. No containment attempt of the zone itself. Responding teams handle individual Potestades generated by the imprint.

## Connection to Kaspar

Hartford zone was active during Kaspar's AMAT custody period (~1982). The Principado's imprint and Kaspar's extended Domain use run parallel as themes of long-term accumulation.
