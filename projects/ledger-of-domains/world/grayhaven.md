# Grayhaven

## Location

Coastal city, New England (Massachusetts implied). Financial district, industrial zone, Breakwater as working-class dormitory neighborhood.

## Atmosphere

Cold metal, sea salt, unstable electrical infrastructure, fragile normality. The city **tolerates** electrical anomalies; people learn not to look.

## Key locations

| Location | Description |
|---|---|
| **Calle Shelter 9, Breakwater** | Oliver's address. Ground floor = Gary's closed repair business. Second floor = living quarters. Attic = Oliver's space. |
| **Electrical grid** | Infrastructure with recurring failures — "faults that don't appear, they behave." Context for GRH-001. |

## Thematic role

Entry point. The blackout that closes Arc 1 is not an ending — it is the first consequence. Everything after Grayhaven has Grayhaven as its origin.
