## Domain — Electricidad

Bauer converts her own Odem into electrical current and can redirect existing electrical infrastructure. Two distinct modes:

**Generation:** produces electrical current from Odem directly. Requires Odem expenditure proportional to output. Useful in environments with no existing grid.

**Redirection:** channels and redirects current already present in existing infrastructure — far more efficient, since she amplifies rather than generates. Requires an active or dormant electrical network to work with.

**Hartford-specific capability:** Hartford's grid has been distorted by proximity to the Odem-saturated zone for decades. The distortion patterns in the current are non-random — they carry information about the field. Bauer has spent five years learning to read these patterns. The distorted current tells her things about the Odem distribution that localizadores don't register. Not a precise map — complementary signal.

**Hartford-specific constraint:** the saturated field means discharges above a certain threshold agitate the ambient Odem in unpredictable ways. Bauer operates with a hard ceiling on output during Hartford operations. Hendricks briefs every team: no discharge above calibration threshold inside the perimeter.

## Personality

Precise. Calibrated. Five years in Hartford have given her very specific knowledge of what the grid can and cannot carry. Tends to give technical assessments rather than tactical ones — she tells you what the current says, not what to do about it.

## Narrative function in BSA

Arc 1 (Hartford). Secondary detection method when localizadores fail. Her grid readings provide a complementary signal to Reyes's substrate sensing — different data, same gap in the standard system.
