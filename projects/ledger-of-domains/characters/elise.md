## Elise = Caso 3 (canon)

Voss has three documented Registry cases across 80 years. The third: worked for Geneva 11 years, retired ~2014 for undocumented medical reasons. Voss knew this person personally. Kaspar observed Elise for eleven years — the numbers match.

She is that case. **Voss knows the outcome. Oliver does not.**

Consequence: when Voss asks for "everything" in Sion, he already knows the only comparable trajectory to Oliver's. Oliver does not know that Voss knows. Voss does not know that Oliver knows her name.

<!-- canonical -->
- Whether Elise and Caso 3 are the same person: intentionally open as of Cap 37. Confirmed in authorial notes, not yet confirmed in prose.

## What is not known (by design)

- What happened to her over those eleven years
- Whether she is still alive
- Whether the Registry consumed her, transformed her, or simply exhausted her
- Whether Kaspar tried to help or only observed
- The "undocumented medical reasons" for her 2014 retirement

## Narrative function

Elise is the mirror Oliver cannot read directly. The Registry leaves no signature when inactive — he cannot copy anyone, cannot see another carrier's trajectory in real time. All he has is Kaspar's word.

**Why she matters in Libro 2:** Oliver made the decision to stay without knowing what that means long-term. Elise is the only evidence it happened before. The question of what happened to her is the question of what will happen to him.
