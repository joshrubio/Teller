## Domain — Odem dissolution

On sustained physical contact, Morales dissolves the structural coherence of Odem in whatever she is touching. Not a suppression — she does not block or freeze Odem function. She unravels it. The Odem's internal structure breaks down under her hands, progressively, for as long as contact is maintained.

Applied to Potestades: the Odem that gives the entity form and function degrades. Abilities fail not because they are blocked but because the substrate that supports them is collapsing. A Potestad in sustained contact with Morales loses coherence from the inside out.

Applied to human agents: same mechanism. Her Domain does not discriminate between Potestad Odem and human Domain Odem. She avoids extended contact with allies for this reason.

**Distinction from Hendricks:** Hendricks blocks active channeling within a radius. Morales destroys Odem structure on contact. Hendricks works at range and is reversible — drop the field, everything reactivates. Morales's dissolution is not reversible while contact holds, and leaves residual degradation after contact breaks. Different tools, different targets.

**Limitation:** requires contact. She cannot operate at range. In Hartford, approaching something the localizador does not read requires trusting her flanks entirely.

**Paired with Brandt:** Morales dissolves structural coherence; Brandt's implosion collapses what remains once it has lost integrity. Against diffuse underground presences with minimal physical form, Morales can contact the emerging edge and begin dissolution — enough degradation for Brandt to implode cleanly.

## Personality

Economical. Eleven years of field work have made her calibrate trust fast — she reads the people she has to get close to before she commits to proximity. Does not telegraph her movements. No unnecessary motion.

## Narrative function in BSA

Arc 1 (Hartford). The operational pair with Brandt. Her Domain is the reason Hendricks can run Morales+Brandt against underground or non-physical entities — she has to get close, but once she does, the entity's Odem architecture is offline.
