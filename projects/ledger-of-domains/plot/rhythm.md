# Rhythm and Structure

Planning reference for chapter pacing and POV decisions.

## Word count by arc

| Arc | Chapters | Words | Avg/chapter |
|---|---|---|---|
| Arc 1 | 1–6 | 15,670 | 2,612 |
| Arc 2 | 7–20 | 38,124 | 2,723 |
| Arc 3 | 21–36 | 48,314 | 3,020 |
| **Book 1 total** | **36** | **~102,108** | **2,836** |

Average rises with each arc — not inflation, but more simultaneous layers per chapter.

**Outlier:** Cap 20 (4,292) — longest. Closes Arc 2, opens Arc 3. Carries two arcs of weight.

**Shortest chapters carry the most semantic load:** Cap 6 (2,006, Park's farewell), Cap 36 (2,475, final decision). Compression is semantic.

## POV pattern — surgical, always provides information Oliver cannot have

| POV | Chapters | Function |
|---|---|---|
| Oliver | ~30 | Intimacy, subjective cost of Registro |
| Park | 5, 6, 19 | External view of damage; politics behind the system |
| Voss | 7, 9, 21, 30 | Institutional mechanics; how AMAT sees what Oliver cannot see about himself |
| Tomás | 26 | Only chapter from outside AMAT — Kaspar without Registro filter |
| Omniscient | 24 | First and only in Book 1. Social scene without fixed protagonist. Deliberate: maps the group when no one is being observed. |
