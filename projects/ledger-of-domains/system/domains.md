## Advanced use — documented but not publicly known to AMAT

**Lev / Calor — advanced (undocumented):** can extract from an object's Odem, consuming the substance maintaining its existence — produces real heat from nothing; the object loses Odem and becomes unanchored (similar effects to Erasure). Long-term cost if he uses his own Odem as reserve. Irreversible.
