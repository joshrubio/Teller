## Mechanic — Sustento

Extracts micro-Odem from living organisms within radius → converts to biological stasis for itself. No organism dies. No organism detects the drain.

Observable secondary effects: nearby living things age slightly faster; animals avoid the area; plants wither.

**Why it is an ontological threat classification:** the boundary between a Domain user and an emergent Potestad erodes under sustained use. Kaspar is the documented 40-year case.

## The copy event (~1982)

Kaspar was approximately 25–30 years old, in AMAT custody, when the Domain was copied. Conditions of the copy are undocumented — which conditions of the Registry were met is not recorded in available files.

17 months later Kaspar disappeared from custody. The Potestad source is listed as neutralized. Cross-referencing the two events is in the AMAT record but no analysis document exists.

## For Oliver

The Odem signature of this Domain (as carried by Kaspar) has been active for 40 years and is indistinguishable from a long-term Registry accumulation. Oliver cannot resolve which layers of Kaspar's signature correspond to Sustento, to Apariencia, or to the Registry itself.
