# Anomaly Classification

Ascending scale: **4 → 3 → 2 → 1 → S**

| Level | Name | Nature | Consciousness | AMAT Protocol |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **4** | Minor | Accumulated Odem distortion in place or object. Does not act — it *is* the anomaly. | None | Contain or ignore |
| **3** | Moderate | Basic impulse without reasoning. Self-preservation, territorial expansion, stimulus response. | Instinctive — animal without language | Neutralize or contain |
| **2** | High | Self-aware as entity. Learns, distinguishes individuals, communicates deliberately. Quasi-negotiable via behavior. | Strategic — primate/cetacean | Contain with caution |
| **1** | Critical | Transmits meaning deliberately. Has persistent objectives. AMAT uses quasi-diplomacy. | Semi-rational | Quasi-diplomacy |
| **S** | Principado | Fully conscious. Philosophy and intentions spanning centuries. AMAT does not contain — negotiates or avoids. | Conscious — may have an opinion about Oliver | Monitor / avoid contact |

> The scale also applies to human Domains (estimated potential capacity).

## Principados — Level S

AMAT operational name for the highest category. Taken from Pauline vocabulary organizing the hierarchy (Colossians 1:16, Ephesians 6:12).

- **Frequency:** 6 events documented by AMAT since WWII.
- **Protocol:** AMAT does not contain. Does not neutralize. Monitors and avoids direct contact. Negotiation only from Geneva's highest level.
- **Legacy:** a Principado that manifests and withdraws leaves the local Odem *impregnated* — can last decades or centuries. Spontaneously generates weak Potestades, distorts how human Domains function in the area.
