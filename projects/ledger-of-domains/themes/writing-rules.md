# Thematic Writing Rules

Load in every write task. These are constraints, not reference.

## Core rule

**Never state a theme directly in prose.** Surface as texture. If the theme can be named without losing narrative information, it is not doing its job.

## Cost rule

The cost of power is always **specific and physical** — never "se sintió cansado." The text names the exact point where the body pays.

## Weight rule

Do not underline the weight of irreversible decisions with excessive internal narration. The reader sees it. Trust.

## Motif table — surface as texture, not statement

## Loyalty rule

Affection is almost always oblique. Do not name what characters feel toward each other — show what they do instead. The reader carries the weight.
